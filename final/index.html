Hanksville, ID

Hanksville, ID is our little slice of heaven nestled in Outlet Bay on Priest Lake, ID. Family owned and operated, Hankville became a place for family and friends to enjoy Idaho's Crown Jewel, Priest Lake. Hank and Arlene Clauson bought this land in 1976 and turned it into a place of good company and beauty. We hope to share our love of Priest Lake with you and look forward to the opportunity for you to make memories here.

With love, the Hanksville Family
