    Host 2 can be found here

  • Sitebuilder.com also offers a free year of domain registraion.
  • They offer three 2-year hosting plans.
  • The Pro plan is $4.00/mo.
  • The Premium plan is $4.80/mo.
  • The ecommerce plan is $7.20/mo.
  • Tech support is offered around the clock, and their A. I. builder will get you started.
  • Another perk is their free library of images to use on your website.
  • They also have marketing tools for your business.

    • Host 3 can be found here

  • Justhost.com comes with a free domain name for one year, or you can transfer your own for free. Also, there is the opportunity to host unlimited domains with one of their unlimited plans.
  • There are 4 hosting plans.
  • The Basic plan comes in at $3.95/mo.
  • The Plus plan is currently $6.95/mo.
  • The Choice Plus plan is a bargain right now at $6.95/mo.
  • Then, they go on to offer the Go Pro plan for $14.99/mo.
  • Justhost.com offers many features such as app installations. ex: WordPress, Sitebuilder, OS Commerce install.
  • They offer a free email, databases, multimedia features, and eCommerce features.

    • After weighing the options, I would choose justhost.com. Although bluehost.com had the most appealing and straightforward website, I feel like I would be just as happy choosing justhost.com. It seems like a simple process with a variety of plans that readily tell you all the features. It is also a cheaper option considering and comparing the plans from the other sites.
